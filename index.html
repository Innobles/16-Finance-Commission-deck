The Problem Landscape

What the 15th Finance Commission Highlighted

India's ₹5.7 Lakh Crore transfer to local bodies faces systemic bottlenecks that erode impact and accountability.

Chronic Delays

Funds get stuck in State treasuries. Local bodies cannot prove readiness or submit Utilization Certificates (UCs) on time.

Zero Visibility

No way for central/state governments to verify if money spent actually resulted in asset creation on the ground.

Idle Float

₹Thousands of Crores sit unspent in local body accounts while projects are delayed or cancelled — invisible to the government.

How AI Validation Works

A simple 5-step explanation — no jargon, just how it keeps things honest.

1
📸

Engineer Takes a Photo

The Junior Engineer at the construction site opens our app and takes a photo of the work (e.g., a completed foundation). The photo can only be taken through the app — no old photos from gallery allowed.

2
📍

Phone Stamps the Location & Time

The moment the photo is taken, the app automatically records the exact GPS location, date, time, and project ID — like a digital fingerprint that can't be faked.

3
🗺️

AI Checks: "Is this the right place?"

Our AI takes the GPS coordinates from the photo and compares them against the approved project site on the map. If someone tries to submit a photo from a different location — the system flags it instantly.

Think of it like this: It's like Google Maps verifying you're actually at the restaurant before you can leave a review.
4
📋

Auto-Generated Progress Report

Once verified, the system automatically creates a professional progress report — "Foundation work 100% complete" — with the photo evidence attached. No one has to write reports manually anymore.

5
💰

Payment Gets Triggered

The report goes to the supervisor's dashboard. They see the evidence, click "Approve" with their Digital Signature, and the system automatically sends a payment request to Axis Bank. No physical paperwork. No delays.

The Bottom Line

If someone claims work is done but hasn't physically been to the site and taken a fresh photo — the system won't allow the payment. Period. This is what makes it fraud-proof.
